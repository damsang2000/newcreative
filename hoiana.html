Hoiana Shores Golf Villas is the latest to join the reputed list of internationally-recognized hospitality brands, namely Hoiana Hotel & Suites, New World Hoiana, KHOS Hoiana, and Rosewood Hoi An. Nestled amongst the fairways, blending perfectly into the relaxing coastal environment and its rolling dunes, Hoiana Shores Golf Villas is an enclave of 88 Golf Villas spanning across a total land size of 13.3 ha with a stunning view of the East Sea and Cham Island.
